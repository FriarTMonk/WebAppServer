- **Parents:** Discipline, faith formation, blended families

- **Men:** Few posts speak specifically to men's issues

### SEO Opportunities
- High-search, low-competition keywords: "Christian dating advice," "biblical money management," "anger management for Christians"
- Long-tail keywords: "How to forgive when they're not sorry," "Is depression a sin," "Should Christians take antidepressants"

### User Journey Mapping
- **Awareness stage:** Bible verses for [problem], What does the Bible say about [topic]
- **Consideration stage:** Biblical principles for [issue], Christian perspective on [struggle]
- **Decision stage:** How to [action], Steps to [outcome], Practical [solution]
